#### Research Project | CoMamba: Coupled Multimodal-Temporal Learning for Incomplete Multimodal Conversation UnderstandingUnderstanding

2025.02—2025.10

- Conducted multimodal sentiment analysis research under the guidance of [Professor Feiyu Chen](https://yjsjy.uestc.edu.cn/gmis/jcsjgl/dsfc/dsgrjj/21450?yxsh=01) at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, addressing the issue of missing multimodal signals (text/audio/visual) in real-world dialogues and the difficulty of capturing entangled semantics with decoupled modeling methods.
- Contributed to the design of the CoMamba model, introducing State Space Models (SSM) into multimodal sentiment analysis. The model integrates Mamba modeling with a rotational scanning mechanism to jointly model temporal dynamics and cross-modal interactions, effectively solving information reconstruction and semantic fusion under modality-missing conditions.
- Achieved state-of-the-art performance on CMU-MOSEI and IEMOCAP datasets under various modality-missing settings. The related work has been submitted to *Pattern Recognition* and a national invention patent has been filed.
